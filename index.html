Hi! I’m Lu Tian, a medical sociologist on a journey toward becoming a scientist in aging research. I explore how social determinants shape health disparities, with a focus on caregiving, social support, health behaviors, and biological aging. I’m passionate about using up-to-date statistical methods to deepen our understanding of aging and extend healthy lifespan.

When I’m not analyzing data or discussing social patterns, I find joy in staying connected, learning from others, and letting curiosity and connection guide my work.

Research Interests

Social determinants of health disparities; aging and the life course; biomarkers; chronic stressors; and social networks.
